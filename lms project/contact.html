Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with a course?

+

Simply create an account, browse our course catalog, and enroll in any course that interests you. You can start learning immediately after enrollment.

What if I need technical support?

+

Our technical support team is available 24/7. You can reach us via email at support@algoforgestudios.com or through the contact form above.

Do you offer refunds?

+

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your course, you can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

Can I access courses offline?

+

Currently, our courses require an internet connection. However, we're working on offline capabilities for select courses.

Do you offer certificates?

+

Yes! Upon completion of any course, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can add to your resume or LinkedIn profile.

Are there any prerequisites?

+

Most of our beginner courses require no prior experience. Advanced courses may have prerequisites, which are clearly listed on each course page.
